Chloe's Movie Ranking List

Top 3 Horrors OF ALL TIME

(in my opinion)

US by Jordan Peele

This psychological horror follows the story of Adelaide Wilson and her family, who are attacked by a group of doppelgangers, later announced as "Tethered". Words cannot describe the creepiness and tension that follows this family, chaos seems to be around every corner.

A highly recommended watch if you need something that keeps you on edge and shocks you with each plot twist it throws at you.

Vivarium by Lorcan Finnegan

Now this, will blow your mind. In the sense of completely confusing the fuck out of you for the first hour. It follows the story of a couple who are forced to care for a mysterious humanoid creature while trapped in a strange, vivarium-like neighbourhood. Each house is a perfect mimic of one-another. Attempting to leave said house would awaken a problem for this couple.

Make sure you are fully awake for this one, many things happen within the span of an hour and it'd be a shame if you missed any of it.

The Silence Of The Lambs by Jonathan Demme

this absolute masterpiece created back in 1991 follows the story if Clarice Starling, a young FBI trainee who is hunting a serial killer named "Buffalo Bill", who skins his female victims. To catch him, she seeks the advice of the imprisoned Hannibal Lector, a brilliant psychiatrist and cannibalistic serial killer.

A cult classic, regularly cited by critics, film directors, and audiences as one of the greatest and most influential films. Need I say more? GO WATCH IT NOW!
